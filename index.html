Our Artisan Legacy

Crafting Heritage, One Step at a Time

Shailly Styles was started in 2025 in Surat, Gujarat, by a passionate housewife and her student partner. What began as a small idea has grown into a heartfelt mission — to celebrate India’s textile traditions through simple, honest fashion.

We are at the very beginning of our journey, learning, exploring, and creating with love. Each piece we design reflects our appreciation for handmade beauty, cultural roots, and timeless style.

While we’re not collaborating with artisans yet, our dream is to grow slowly and meaningfully — supporting local craftsmanship and making fashion that feels personal, ethical, and proud of its heritage.
